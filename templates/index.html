About :

Alpha - Text Data Analyzer

Alpha is a text data analyzer designed to provide insights into news articles from The Indian Express. Users can input the URL of any Indian Express article, and Alpha will perform text analysis, giving details such as the number of sentences, words, POS tags, cleaned text, title of the article, and more.

Explore the depth of news articles and gain valuable information with Alpha. Stay informed and analyze articles like never before.
